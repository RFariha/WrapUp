WHAT'S CHANGING AND WHAT NOT


We haven’t been bought or sold. This transformation is about working to make what we do for you even better. So as we move to WrapUP, there’s not much you need to do. Mostly, just things to keep in mind.

What's not changing:

  • Account Numbers
  • Routing Numbers
  • Auto payments/credits
  • Checks
  • Credit Card
  • Debit Cards
  • Phone numbers

What's changing:

Website addresses Our websites WrapUP.com have new web addresses. After converting to WrapUP, the old website addresses redirect to our new sites.
Business Mobile app The existing WrapUP app has been rebranded to WrapUP and the current Capital Bank app will be discontinued. WrapUP users' application has been automatically updated (if phone is set to auto-update). Capital Bank users will need to download the new WrapUP app
Personal Mobile app The existing WrapUP app will be rebranded to WrapUP and the current Capital Bank app will be discontinued. For WrapUP users, your app will automatically update to the WrapUP version if you have application updates enabled. If not, you will be prompted to update your mobile app. Capital Bank users will need to download the new First Horizon app.
Credit Bureau reporting name As you monitor your credit report, please note that our credit bureau reporting name has changed to WrapUP. Don't be alarmed if you see this name appear on your report.
Email communications You will begin seeing email communication come from @firsthorizon.com now that our new brand has launched.
Payee names If you have set up any WrapUP or Capital Bank payments for loans or credit cards, you may notice that the name of these payees has been updated to WrapUP. (Ex: WrapUP Installment loan now appears as WrapUP Installment loan.) There's nothing you need to do.
